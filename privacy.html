Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how The Hub ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares your information when you use our website and services.

Information We Collect

We may collect various types of information including:

  • Personal Information: Such as your name, email address, or contact details provided voluntarily.
  • Usage Information: Like your IP address, browser type, and device information automatically collected as you interact with our website.
  • Cookies and Tracking Technologies: We use cookies and similar technologies to track your activity and store certain information.

How We Use Your Information

We may use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain our services.
  • Communicate with you and offer customer support.
  • Personalize your experience and enhance our website.
  • Send updates and promotional materials (with your consent).
  • Comply with legal obligations.

Sharing Your Information

We may share your information with:

  • Service Providers: Who help in operating our website and services.
  • Business Partners: For joint initiatives and marketing purposes.

Your Choices

You have several choices regarding your data:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal details by contacting us.
  • Opt-out of receiving promotional emails with provided instructions.
  • Disable cookies in your browser (note that this may impact your user experience).

Cookie Policy

Cookies are small files stored on your device. We use them to:

  • Remember your preferences and enhance your website experience.
  • Analyze site traffic and usage patterns.
  • Deliver relevant advertising content.

You can control cookie usage via your browser settings. Learn more by visiting About Cookies.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data.
  • Request corrections or updates to your data.
  • Request deletion of your data subject to applicable laws.

To exercise these rights, please contact our support team.

Third-Party Services

Our website may contain links or integrations with third-party services (analytics, social media, etc.). We are not responsible for their data practices. Please review their privacy policies before using these services.

Security

We implement reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, but please note that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may modify this Privacy Policy periodically. If significant changes occur, we will notify you by posting an updated version on this page.
